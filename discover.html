At Basil Group, we believe in the maxim ‘the extraordinary begins where the ordinary ends.’

Steering away from stereotyped luxury developments, Basil Group works with a singular focus to deliver luxury that is truly next level.

We craft landmarks that epitomize grandeur and opulence in every square foot. Our exceptionally high standards of design, craftsmanship, and project implementation lead to the creation of masterpieces that easily rise above the clutter.

Our inspired journey spanning 17 years is marked by many milestones of success. ‘Basil Maximus’ and ‘Basil Vrindavan’, our two signature projects, have clearly emerged as the emblem of exclusivity and extravagance in Pune’s realty landscape.
