FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

Where is Aqua Arc located?

+

Aqua Arc is located at the Al Marjan Island in the idyllic Ras Al Khaimah. Set amidst the tranquil shores, the strategic location of the property provides effortless navigation and accessibility to major points of the city.

How accessible is Al Marjan Island?

+

Al Marjan Island is highly accessible owing to its proximity to major transportation joints. It connects you to the important landmarks of the city and the property in itself is approximately 45-minutes away from Dubai International Airport.

What types of properties are available and in which sizes?

+

Catering to a diverse range of needs and requirements and with 226 units available in its G+12 twin towers, you get everything from opulent penthouses to luxury 1 bedroom sea view apartment at Marjan Island. While 1BR apartments are sized at 1,030.97 - 1,729.44 Sq. Ft., 2BR apartments are available in 1,496.40 - 1,975.72 Sq. Ft. whereas 3BR Apartments come in a size of 2,197.99 - 4,589.62 Sq. Ft. You also get luxury townhouses sized at 2,466.66 - 2,510.79 Sq. Ft and penthouses at 8,659.14 - 8,660.97 Sq. Ft.

When is the handover of the Aqua Arc project?

+

The expected handover for Aqua Arc at Al Marjan Island is in the third quarter of 2027.

What is the expected capital appreciation and RoI?

+

Aqua Arc comes with immense potential due to its strategic advantages. The expected economic growth on the island is set to contribute to its high-end potential as well. So, you can expect a very lucrative annual yield from this project. Moreover, there is no capital gain tax and corporate tax in the area, making it a lucrative investment option.

What is the starting price and minimum down payment?

+

The luxurious properties at Aqua Arc start at AED 2.3 million. With a flexible and economical payment plan package, you can book a property with a down payment of just 10%, making it extremely easy to plan your finances while buying a property on this beautiful island.
